How to Use Career Hub

What is Career Hub?

Career Hub is your personal job hunt dashboard. It helps you track companies you're interested in, save customized bookmarks with filters applied, manage contacts, and monitor your daily outreach progress.

Core Features

1. Add & Track Companies

Click "Add company" to save any company you're interested in. Add a name, URL, tag (e.g., "Tech", "Startup"), and optional notes. All data is saved locally in your browser.

2. Customized Bookmarks (The Main Idea)

The key feature: Save company pages with filters already applied. For example, if you find a job listing page with filters for "Remote" and "Senior" roles, save that link. Next time you visit, those filters are already set — no need to reapply them.

3. Daily Visit Tracking

Click on a company card to visit its URL. Each visit is recorded for today. The "Today" counter at the top shows how many companies you visited today vs. your total companies.

4. Search & Filter

Use the search bar to find companies by name or notes. Click tags to filter by category. Use "All" to reset filters.

5. Manage Contacts

Click "Add contact" to save recruiters, hiring managers, or referrals. Assign them to companies, add email, phone, LinkedIn, and notes. Click a company to view all contacts for that company.

6. Visit History

Click the calendar button to see your visit history across months and years. Plan your outreach and stay consistent with your job hunt.

7. Personalization

Click the "Career Hub" title to add your name. This appears at the top and makes the dashboard feel more personal.

8. LinkedIn Integration

Click the LinkedIn logo (top right) to save your LinkedIn profile URL. Click it again anytime to quickly open your profile.

9. Export & Backup

Click "Export" to download all your data (companies, contacts, name, LinkedIn URL) as a JSON file. Keep this safe for backups or migrating to another device.

10. Import Data

Click "Import" to:
• Upload Chrome/Edge bookmarks (HTML file) to add multiple companies at once
• Upload a previous backup (JSON file) to restore your data

⚠️ Important Cautions

🕵️ Avoid Incognito Mode: Do not use Career Hub in incognito or private browsing mode. These modes block local storage, meaning all your data will be lost the moment you close the window. Always use a regular browser tab for the best experience.

🔒 Data Storage: Everything is stored locally in your browser. If you clear your browser cookies or site data, you will lose all data. Always export and backup regularly.

📱 Device-Specific: Your data is not synced across devices. If you use Career Hub on multiple devices, export from one and import on another to keep data in sync.

🕐 Timezone: All dates and times follow your system's local timezone. Make sure your device timezone is set correctly.

🚀 No Backend: Career Hub is completely offline and private. No data is sent to any server. You have 100% privacy.

Quick Tips

  • Visit at least 1 company per day to build momentum
  • Use tags to organize by industry, company size, or stage
  • Add detailed notes for follow-up reminders
  • Export your data weekly as a backup habit
  • On mobile, use the hamburger menu (☰) to access all buttons
