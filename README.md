# Career Hub

> A personal job-hunt dashboard that lives entirely in your browser — no backend, no account, no build step.

🌐 **Live at [careerhub.lol](https://careerhub.lol)**

---

## What is Career Hub?

Career Hub is a free, private tool for organizing your job search. Save company career pages with your filters already applied, track daily visits, manage recruiter contacts, and stay consistent — all without switching between 10 different tabs.

No sign-up. No server. No tracking. Just you and your job search.

---

## Features

### 🏢 Company Tracking

- Add any company with a name, career page URL, tag, and personal note
- Edit, remove, or drag and drop to reorder companies
- Import from Chrome/Edge bookmarks (HTML) or a previous backup (JSON)
- Export all data as JSON for backup or device transfer

### 📅 Daily Visit Tracking

- Click "Open careers" on any tile to visit and auto-record it
- Progress bar shows how many companies you visited today vs. your total
- Click the visited chip to undo an accidental visit

### 🗓 Visit History Calendar

- See which days you were active
- Monthly tally of total visits
- Today's date highlighted

### 👥 Contact Management

- Save recruiters, hiring managers, and referrals
- Store name, email, phone, LinkedIn, role, and notes
- Map contacts directly to companies
- Contacts sorted alphabetically per company

### 🔍 Search & Filter

- Search across company names, tags, and notes simultaneously
- Filter by tag with one click
- Search and filter work together

### 🔖 Customized Bookmarks

- Save career pages with filters already applied
- Next visit — your filters are already set, no need to reapply

### 🎨 Personalization

- Click the title to add your name
- Browser tab title updates too
- Stored locally — only you see it

### 💼 LinkedIn Quick Access

- Save your LinkedIn profile URL once
- Click anytime to jump directly to your profile

### 📊 US Job Market Pulse

- Quick link to a live US job market visualizer
- Check macro hiring trends without leaving your workflow

### 🔒 Privacy First

- Zero backend — all data lives in your browser only
- Nothing is ever sent to any server
- Your data is 100% yours

## How to Use It

1. **Get started** — Visit [careerhub.lol](https://careerhub.lol) — no sign-up needed
2. **Add companies** — Add manually or import your existing bookmarks
3. **Save customized links** — Bookmark career pages with filters already applied
4. **Track daily progress** — Mark companies as visited each day
5. **Manage contacts** — Save recruiters and hiring managers per company
6. **Search & filter** — Find companies instantly by name, tag, or note
7. **Export regularly** — Back up your data as JSON to avoid losing it

---

## ⚠️ Important

- **Avoid incognito mode** — private browsing blocks local storage, you will lose all data when the window closes
- **Clear site data = data loss** — clearing cookies and site data will erase everything. Clearing cache alone is safe
- **Not synced across devices** — export from one device and import on another to transfer data
- **Backup regularly** — click Export anytime to download a JSON backup

---

## Privacy

Every session is completely private. Your data never leaves your device and is never sent to any server. If someone else visits careerhub.lol, they see a blank slate — your data is yours alone.
